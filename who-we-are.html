Who we are

MEDDY GLOBAL SOLUTIONS LTD is a 100% Nigerian company with operational base in Lagos, Nigeria. The company has been providing services in areas of oil and gas sector, pipeline and production facilities engineering, procurement and logistics services, environmental studies, waste management/pollution Control. Our Expertise lies in the identification of supplies, goods, and services, the negotiation of pricing, terms, and conditions, and in delivery to the end user.

MEDDY GLOBAL SOLUTIONS LTD is an exceptional procurement service provider, helping organization address all parts of the earning and sourcing operations to achieve savings and improve supplier relationships. With decades building strategic sourcing best practices and bleeding edge procurement processes, MEDDY GLOBAL SOLUTIONS LTD has helped many organizations realized billions of hard dollar savings in the procured products and services. While doing so, WE helped to increase efficiency, responsiveness and flexibly in those supply chains.

Our company is made up of Proficient and seasoned professional business administrators, especially for the purpose of providing varied technical services to the oil/gas industry. We Render services to the upstream and downstream sectors of the oil & gas industry, delivers quality using well-trained and tested professional workforce, utilizes modern technology, proven engineering techniques and tested management tools to assure our customers' continuous delight and satisfaction.
