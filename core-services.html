Safety Health And Environment (HSE)

At MEDDY GLOBAL SOLUTIONS LTD, we regard safety, health and environmental performance to be of prime importance to our business and we are highly committed to continual improvement in these areas of all our operations.We make it a culture to train and enlighten our workforce on the safety requirements of all our activities.We have consistent and stringent framework for the management of safety, health and environmental issues to help us achieve our safety standards.We measure safety performance through audit procedures and benchmark indicators to identify areas of improvement.

HSE Policy

HSE POLICY It is our policy to secure the health and safety of our employees, contractors, other members of the public as well as the environment, plant and equipment in all our work locations, None of our business objectives is so important that it will be pursued at the sacrifice of safety. Every member of our work force will follow each job procedures consistent with applicable safety standard. Each and every member of the work force has a personal responsibility for our safety and the safety of others both at on and off the job.

Quality Management Policy

We use Total Quality Management approach as a way of life and the quality system is a company-wide basis of our operations. In this approach, processes are defined, developed, acknowledged and above all, they are logically implemented.

Community Policy

MEDDY GLOBAL SOLUTIONS LTD recognizes that its activities have an immediate Impact on the communities in which we work. We will minimize that by adopting the behaviors laid down under the considerate constructor scheme by being a good neighbor, engaging in constructive dialogue and work in partnership with the community, promote education and training, respect and acknowledge local cultural and religious needs, respond promptly to enquiries from interested parties and provide relevant information regarding our activities.
